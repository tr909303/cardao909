ABOUT

Cardão is a passionate electronic music enthusiast who began to develop a strong interest in the genre around 2007, at the age of 16. His early influences included DJs like Jeffmills, CarlCox and the Underground Sound of Lisbon. He began to play and perform DJ sets, showcasing a variety of styles that are sure to get audiences dancing. As he attended more parties and events, he became increasingly drawn to techno, with a particular fascination for the sounds of Detroit. This led to a shift in his DJ sets, which became more powerful, raw, mental and industrial. Cardão's major influences include Jeff Mills, Robert Hood, Surgeon, Oscar Mulero, Truncate, Pfirter, and Deepbass, among others. These artists have played a significant role in shaping his unique sound and approach to electronic music.
